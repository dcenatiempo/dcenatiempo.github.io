Privacy Policy

For Hermes-Google-Integration · Last updated June 12, 2026

Overview

Hermes-Google-Integration ("the App") is a personal desktop application that lets an AI agent ("Hermes") access and act on the operator's own Google account through Google APIs. The App is installed and run by an individual on their own computer, and it operates only on the Google account that the same individual personally authorizes. There is no shared service, multi-tenant backend, or central database operated by us. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Information the App Accesses

With the operator's explicit consent during Google sign-in, the App may request access to the following Google services, depending on the scopes the operator grants:

  • Gmail — reading, searching, drafting, sending, and managing email messages.
  • Google Calendar — reading and managing calendar events.
  • Google Drive, Docs, and Sheets — reading and managing files and documents.
  • Basic profile information — name, email address, and account identifier used for authentication.

The App only accesses data necessary to carry out the specific tasks the operator asks the agent to perform.

How Information Is Used

Information accessed through Google APIs is used solely to fulfill requests the operator makes to the agent — for example, searching email, summarizing a document, drafting a reply, or managing calendar events. The data is not used for advertising, is not sold, and is not used to build profiles for any purpose unrelated to the operator's own requests.

How Information Is Processed and Shared

Google data is processed locally on the operator's own device. To fulfill a request, relevant portions of that data may be sent to a third-party large language model (LLM) provider that the operator chooses and configures — for example, Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI, or Google (Gemini) — or to a model the operator runs locally, in which case no data leaves the device. When a remote provider is used, the data is transmitted to that provider only to generate a response for the operator's request, and the provider processes it under its own privacy policy and terms. The operator is solely responsible for choosing which provider, if any, to enable.

Aside from the operator-chosen LLM provider used to fulfill a request, the App does not transfer Google user data to any other third party.

Limited Use Disclosure

Hermes-Google-Integration's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Data Storage and Retention

OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored locally on the operator's device so the App can connect to Google without repeated sign-in. Google data fetched to complete a request is held only in memory or local working storage for as long as needed to complete that request, and is not retained in any central system operated by us. The operator can delete the App's local data and tokens at any time by removing the App's stored credentials from their device.

Revoking Access

The operator can revoke the App's access to their Google account at any time via the Google Account permissions page at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Security

Because the App runs locally and stores credentials on the operator's own device, the security of that data depends on the security of the device and the operator's chosen configuration. We recommend running the App only on trusted, access-controlled machines.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
